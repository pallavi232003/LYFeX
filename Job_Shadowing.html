The LYFeX Job Shadowing Program offers an observation-based learning experience to help students explore various career paths. The program is focused on developing cognitive skills and helping students gain clarity about their future career choices. It encourages self-learning, allowing students to uncover their true interests and make informed decisions regarding their career direction.
How LYFeX Shadowing Works

Understanding Interests

The LYFeX team meets with students to understand their interests, strengths, and aspirations through one-on-one or group discussions.

Expert Matchmaking

Based on the insights gathered, students are paired with professionals (mentors) in relevant fields for the shadowing experience.

(2 Days)

An introductory orientation prepares students by explaining expectations, behavioral standards, and the shadowing process‚Äîemphasizing observation, self-driven learning, and reflection.

Shadowing Program
(3 Months)

Students engage in observation-based learning, spending time with mentors, and observing daily activities, decision-making processes, and workflows in real-world settings.

‚Å†Self-Learning Focus

Mentors guide the students through observation, not direct teaching. The program is built on self-learning, where students analyze and reflect on what they observe, ask questions, and seek clarifications to deepen their understanding.

Post- Program Reflection

At the end of the 3 months, students reflect on whether the field and mentor align with their interests.
If yes, the program continues for another quarter.
If no, students can explore a different field or mentor in the next cycle.

Continuation

Students can continue the shadowing program until they find their passion and an ideal mentor.

Reporting

LYFeX provides a detailed report to students, parents, and schools, outlining progress and key observations.

Behavioral Analysis

Instead of grades, a behavioral analysis report is shared, focusing on:
Cognitive development
Engagement and initiative
Problem-solving and adaptability

Certificate

At the end of the program, a yearly certificate is awarded, summarizing the student‚Äôs participation, achievements, and skills developed during the program

About LYFeX

LYFeX (Live Your Future Experience) is an innovative program designed to empower students by providing them with early exposure to real-world careers and professional environments. By blending hands-on experiences, mentorship, and emotional development, LYFeX equips young minds to make informed career decisions and develop essential skills for future success.
